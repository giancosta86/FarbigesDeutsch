Weak declension

{% include declension.html declension=site.data.adjectives.weak %}

Attributive adjectives follow this declension when they are after:

  • the definite article (der)
  • dieser
  • jener
  • welcher
  • mancher
  • alle

Mixed declension

{% include declension.html declension=site.data.adjectives.mixed %}

Attributive adjectives follow this declension when they are after:

  • The indefinite article: ein
  • The negative article: kein
  • Possessive articles: mein, dein, sein, ihr, unser, euer, Ihr

Strong declension

{% include declension.html declension=site.data.adjectives.strong %}

Attributive adjectives follow this declension when they are not preceded by articles.

No declension
